About God's_will_ministry Uganda

Our Commitment to Orphaned Children in Mityana

Our Mission

God's_will_ministry Uganda is dedicated to providing comprehensive care, protection, and support to orphaned and vulnerable children in Mityana District. We create safe environments, ensure quality education, provide healthcare, and offer emotional support to help orphans thrive and build hopeful futures.

Our Vision

A Mityana District where every orphaned child has access to shelter, nutrition, education, healthcare, and unconditional love—enabling them to grow into confident, healthy, and contributing members of society.

Our Core Values

  • Love & Compassion: We provide nurturing care and unconditional love to every orphan
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and accountability in all our work
  • Child-Centered Care: Every decision is made with the best interest of orphaned children in mind
  • Dignity & Respect: We honor the rights and dignity of every orphan in our care
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in care, education, and support services

Our Orphan Care Programs

Holistic Support for Vulnerable Children

Safe Shelter & Care Homes

We provide secure, loving homes for orphaned children who have no guardians. Our care homes offer safe shelter, nutritious meals, hygiene facilities, and a family-like environment where children feel protected and cared for.

Learn More →

Quality Education for Orphans

Every orphan in our care attends school with full support—tuition, uniforms, books, and school supplies. We ensure quality education so orphans can pursue their dreams and break the cycle of poverty.

Learn More →

Comprehensive Healthcare

Orphans receive regular medical check-ups, vaccinations, nutrition support, and mental health counseling. We partner with health centers to ensure every orphan grows up healthy and strong.

Learn More →

Nutrition & Food Security

Orphans receive three nutritious meals daily plus healthy snacks. We ensure proper nutrition is never a challenge, supporting their physical and mental development.

Learn More →

Counseling & Emotional Support

We provide trauma-informed counseling and emotional support to help orphans process grief and build resilience. Our caregivers provide unconditional love and mentorship.

Learn More →

Skills Training & Future Planning

As orphans approach adulthood, we provide vocational training, life skills, and employment support to ensure they can become independent, self-sufficient adults.

Learn More →
